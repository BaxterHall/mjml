Preparing Your
Dental Practice For Sale

For most dentists, the sale of their practice will probably be the single largest financial transaction of their professional lives. And, the most complex because of the numerous tax, legal and emotional aspects involved, in addition to the financial ones. The decisions taken at this crucial time will have life-long ramifications.
Drawing on more than 25 years experience of helping dentists through this process, Stive Farronato and Chris Molloy - Senior VPs at TMFD Financial, share their insights and recommendations with Dr. Michael Diston in an informative chat about the whys, hows and whats of Preparing Your Dental Practice for Sale.
Episode 1 of 6 - Why Prepare?
Stive and Chris chat with Dr. Diston about recognizing the need to plan and prepare adequately and in advance of a dental practice sale.
